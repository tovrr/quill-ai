# Quill AI 14-Day Launch Calendar

## Goal
Drive qualified early adoption for Quill Agents using a familiar UX narrative with a clear trust-control differentiator.

Core message: Familiar AI agents. Trusted by design.

## Publishing Rhythm
- Channels: X and LinkedIn
- Daily cadence: 1 primary post + optional reply thread or short follow-up
- Recommended local publish windows:
  - X: 09:30 or 18:00
  - LinkedIn: 08:30 or 12:15

## KPI Targets for the 14-Day Window
- Waitlist conversion from social clicks: >= 8%
- First-session activation (first successful multi-provider run): >= 35%
- Landing page CTA click-through: >= 4%
- Free-to-Pro conversion from launch cohort: >= 6%

## Daily Execution Checklist
- Publish on X and LinkedIn with UTM-tagged links
- Reply to first 10 comments within 2 hours
- Repost with one additional insight 6-8 hours later
- Log metrics in one tracker row per post
- Save top audience objections for pricing and FAQ updates

## Reusable Post Formula
- Hook: 1 line naming a real operator pain
- Insight: 2-3 lines reframing the problem
- Proof: 1 example, screenshot, or metric
- CTA: 1 action only
