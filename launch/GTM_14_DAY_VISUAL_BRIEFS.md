# Quill AI 14-Day Visual Brief Pack

Purpose: one visual brief per day aligned with the GTM calendar and social posts.
Format target: 1080x1080 primary, plus optional 1200x627 variant.
Style direction: intentional, modern, high-contrast red-led Quill palette.

## Global Art Direction
- Primary colors: #EF4444, #DC2626, #0B0B12, #E6E8EC, #6F737A
- Typography direction: geometric sans for headlines, neutral sans for body
- Motif: control overlays, route paths, policy boundaries, signal grids
- Motion (optional): 6-10 second loop with subtle route pulses and boundary highlights
